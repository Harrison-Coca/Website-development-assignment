About Us


Founded in 2020, Roses Pub and Kitchen was born from a love of good food and great company. Located in the heart of Accra, we serve up authentic Ghanaian dishes using fresh, local ingredients. Our team is passionate about offering a warm, welcoming experience and sharing the flavors of Ghana with the world.

Our mission is simple: to bring people together through food and drink, while supporting local farmers and suppliers.
